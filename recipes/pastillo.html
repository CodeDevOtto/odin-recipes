Pastillo

Description

Pastillo is a traditional Aragonese pastry, especially popular in rural areas. It consists of a crispy dough filled with sweet pumpkin and sometimes flavored with cinnamon or anise.

Ingredients

  • 500g pumpkin, peeled and grated
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon anise liqueur
  • 300g all-purpose flour
  • 100ml olive oil
  • 100ml white wine
  • 1 pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Mix the grated pumpkin with sugar, cinnamon and anise liqueur.
  3. Let it sit for about 30 minutes to release its juices.
  4. In a bowl, mix the flour, olive oil, white wine and salt.
  5. Knead until smooth and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  6. Roll out the dough into thin circles (about 15cm in diameter).
  7. Place a spoonful of the pumpkin filling in the center.
  8. Fold the dough over to form a half-moon shape and seal the edges by pressing with a fork.
  9. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the pastillos until golden and crispy.
  10. Drain on paper towels.
  11. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving.
